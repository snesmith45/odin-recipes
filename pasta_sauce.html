Italian Pasta Sauce

Description:

The best compliment to your favorite pasta with an aquired taste you wont forget.

Ingredients:

  • 4 pounds of ground turkey
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 celery ribs, finely chops
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 3 cans crushed tomatoes in puree
  • 3 cans tomato paste
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 pound mushrooms, sliced
  • 3/4 cup minced parsley
  • 1 tbs sugar
  • 2-1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp allspice
  • Dont forget your favorite pasta!

Instructions:

  1. Cook turkey over medium heat ; drain and set aside
  2. Saute onion, celery and garlic in oil until tender
  3. Return meat to pan containing vetables and add rest of ingredients.
  4. Cover and simmer until desired thickness
  5. Stir occasionally for 2-3 hours
  6. Season to taste
  7. Serve!

Nutrition Facts

Serving Size - 1 cup: 284 calories, 15g fat (5g saturated fat), 57mg cholesterol, 821mg sodium, 16g carbohydrate (9g sugars, 3g fiber), 23g protein.
