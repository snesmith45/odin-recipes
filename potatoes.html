Colcannon Potatoes

Description

Not your average bowl of mashed potatoes! This popular Irish recipe is filled with goodness and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium head cabbage, shredded
  • 4 pounds of medium potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1 cup chopped green onions
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • Minced fresh parsley
  • crumbled cooked bacon

Directions

  1. Place cabbage in water and bring to boil until tender.
  2. Drain cooking liquid and place cabbage in separate dish.
  3. Combine potatoes and cooking liquid.
  4. Add water to cover potatoes and bring to boil until tender.
  5. Place milk, green onions, salt and pepper in saucepan and bring to boil.
  6. Remove from heat.
  7. Drain potatoes. Place in large bowl and mash.
  8. Add milk for desired texture
  9. Stir in cabbage
  10. Drizzle with butter. Top with parsley and bacon.
  11. Serve.

Nutrition Facts

Serving Size - 1 cup: 168 calories, 5g fat (3g saturated fat), 14mg cholesterol, 361mg sodium, 27g carbohydrate (6g sugars, 4g fiber), 4g protein
