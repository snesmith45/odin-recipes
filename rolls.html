Scottish Oatmeal Rolls

Description:

Scottish classic breakfast treat. Ball of joy you won't be able to get enough.

Ingredients:

  • 1-1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1-1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1-1/2 salt
  • 1 tbs canola oil
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 2-3/4 to 3-1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • Butter
  • Honey

Directions:

  1. Pour boiling water over oats in large bowl.
  2. Add brown sugar, salt, and oil and cool to 110 - 115 degrees.
  3. Turn dough onto floured surface, kneed until smooth and elastic for 6-8 minutes.
  4. Place in greased bowl and turn once to grease top.
  5. Cover, let rise in warm place for 1 hr or until doubled in size.
  6. Punch dough down; divide and shape into 24 balls.
  7. Place in greased baking pan, cover and let rice until doubled.
  8. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  9. Bake until lightly browned, 20-25 minutes.
  10. Remove pan from wire rack to cool.
  11. Serve with butter and honey.

Nutrition Facts

Serving Size: 1 roll: 89 calories, 1g fat (0 saturated fat), 0 cholesterol, 149mg sodium, 17g carbohydrate (3g sugars, 1g fiber), 2g protein.
